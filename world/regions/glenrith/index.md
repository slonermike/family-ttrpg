The wetlands of Glenrith begin at the foot of the Darkened Ridge's western slope and spread outward for miles — old-growth forest, slow rivers, standing water, and the constant smell of things growing and rotting at the same time.

Dorogh's fog has not reached Glenrith. This is not comfort — it is strangeness. The people here know about the shadow over Rjocht. They chose not to run toward it or away from it. They built higher and waited.

The Swamp of Putrid Stank dominates the center of the region — a wide, slow, genuinely terrible-smelling expanse that most travelers avoid. The only reliable way through is the old causeways, and those require knowing where to step. The people of Caedreth know.

Glenrith is not hostile. It is indifferent to newcomers in the specific way that very old places are — it has seen things come through before and it is still here.
