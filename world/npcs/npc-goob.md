> [!DIALOG]
> **Goob:** "...Murn? You're not Murn." A pause. His ears tilt. "Did Murn send you?"

Goob is a Wumble — round, velvet-furred, enormous ears, no eyes. He and :npc[murn] came to the ridge together. He went into the cavern and the floor gave way, stranding him on a ledge above the gap. He's been there since.

He is not panicking. He made a small, neat pile of pebbles he found on the ledge while he waited. He has been quietly confident that someone would come. He's never been wrong about that before.

His ears are slightly different from Murn's (they have a long-running friendly disagreement about whether this matters). Whatever resonance Blind Anger produces didn't affect Goob the same way — he can be in the cave, just not get back down.

## Rescuing Goob

He's on a ledge above an eight-foot gap where the floor gave way. He can help whoever comes for him — his spatial sense from sound is excellent.

**If someone's climbing toward him:** Goob can coach their exact hand and footholds. +2 to any Agility roll made to reach him.

**Rescue options:**
- **Agility 12+** — climb across
- **Might 10+** — throw rope, lower him
- **Creation 10+** — improvise a bridge or catch
- **Wit 12+** — talk him down a route he can navigate himself

## In the Boss Battle

When the reunion with Murn is over, they both disappear into the dark. They come back when it matters.

:npc[murn] and Goob stand at the edge of the chamber during the Blind Anger fight. They track every breath, every footfall, every shift in weight. **Each player gets one free re-roll** (attack or defense) this encounter. When used, describe what Goob whispered just before the moment.
