A round merchant who has made the road between the villages and Rjocht his life for many years. He is one of those people who finds everything interesting and most things delicious. The :item[strange-bun] did not disgust him. He was enthusiastic.

Under Dorogh's oppression, merchants like Jasper have had to walk a careful line — keep trading, keep moving, don't attract attention. He still finds reasons to laugh. Joy is returning to him as he senses something is shifting.

## GM Notes

Jasper knows the roads and gates of Rjocht better than most — merchants figure out the soft spots. He directed the party to the East Market Gate rather than the main entrance.

He has a prior relationship with Jorik (the blacksmith in Rjocht's eastern district). This is a small thread the party could pull — Jasper might know things about the city's interior through Jorik's perspective.

**Voice:** warm, animated, finds every food fascinating. Speaks like he's always on the verge of a good story.

## Session 8 Callback

Jasper arrives in Rjocht after liberation. He brings goods to trade and tears he probably won't admit to.
