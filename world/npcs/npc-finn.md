Finn is everywhere and nowhere. He knows every alley, shortcut, guard rotation, loose stone in the eastern wall, and which market sellers will look the other way. He's been surviving in Rjocht on wits alone long enough that the guards don't really see him anymore — which is exactly how he likes it.

He is not cynical. That's the surprise. He could be, but he isn't. Joy is stubborn in him. He finds things funny even when they aren't, because finding things funny is a survival strategy he developed long ago and now genuinely means.

## GM Notes

Finn is the party's best resource if they want to move through Rjocht without being seen. He can guide, scout, carry messages, or find things.

He will help if the party treats him like a person instead of a tool. He's been used as a tool before.

**Voice:** Fast-talking, light on his feet, drops into sudden seriousness without warning. "I know a way. It's not pretty but it works. You want pretty or you want through?"

**Loyalty trigger:** If the party does something genuinely kind for him or for Rjocht's people, Finn attaches. He will do things for them he wouldn't do for coin.
