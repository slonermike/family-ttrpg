# Weapons & Armor

All weapons and armor restore at end of battle (not consumable unless card states otherwise).

## Armor

| Item | Description | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Armor | Protective gear | When you take damage, flip this face-down instead of flipping an ability card. Restores at end of battle. |

## Weapons

| Item | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Sword | Sharp blade for close combat | Melee attacks, cutting through obstacles |
| Hammer | Heavy weapon that smashes through defenses | Devastating attacks, breaking objects, smashing barriers |
| Axe | Chopping weapon for combat and utility | Fighting enemies, chopping through obstacles |
| Bow & Arrows | Ranged weapon | Attacking from range, hunting |
| Daggers | Quick blades for fast strikes | Quick melee attacks, precise cutting |

All weapons add +d6 to an ability roll when used together (e.g., Might + Sword = roll Might dice + d6 from sword).
