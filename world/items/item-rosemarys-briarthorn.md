name: Rosemary's Briarthorn
type: legendary
tagline: A morningstar that lodges in its target and doesn't let go without a fight.
effect: "Hit: 1 damage; the Briarthorn lodges in the enemy. Each round after: enemy rolls d20. 8 or lower → 1 damage (thorns dig deeper). 9 or higher → weapon dislodges, falls to ground; holder may pick it up and use it again."
consumable: false

A heavy morningstar — iron shaft, iron head, iron spines that curve inward like thorns. The spines aren't straight. They hook. Once they're in, pulling the weapon out tears worse than the entry did.

The name is etched in small letters along the grip, in a hand that looks nothing like a weaponsmith's.

**On a hit:** The Briarthorn lodges in the enemy. Deal 1 damage. The holder no longer holds the weapon — it's in the enemy now.

**Each subsequent round:** The enemy rolls d20. On **8 or lower**, the thorns dig in — 1 damage, weapon stays lodged. On **9 or higher**, the enemy shakes it free — the weapon drops to the ground (or floor, or mud). The holder can pick it up on their next turn and use it again.

**While lodged:** The holder's hands are free. They can use another item, help an ally, or just watch it work.

**Stacking:** The Briarthorn can only be lodged in one enemy at a time. It cannot lodge in a second enemy while already inside the first.
