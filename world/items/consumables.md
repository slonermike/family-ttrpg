# Consumable Items

Items marked CONSUMABLE are permanently removed after use. All others restore at end of battle.

## Healing & Recovery

| Item | Description | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Healing Potion | Glowing red vial | Flip one ability card face-up (self or ally) — CONSUMABLE |
| Greater Healing Potion | Brilliant ruby vial | Flip BOTH ability cards face-up (self or ally) — CONSUMABLE |
| Healer's Kit | Leather bag of medical supplies | Flip one ability card face-up (self or ally) |
| Ward Stone | Smooth stone that glows with protective light | Absorbs the next attack instead of flipping an ability card — CONSUMABLE |
