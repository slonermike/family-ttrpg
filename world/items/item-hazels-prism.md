A hand-sized crystal, dull grey at rest. When it absorbs an attack, its facets light up — ember-orange for fire, cold blue for ice, white for lightning.

**Absorb:** Declare "I absorb it" when hit by an energy attack, then roll d20. 12+ → no damage, gain 1 charge (max 2). 11 or lower → hit lands normally. Can't absorb if already at 2 charges.

**Release:** Attack action — spend all charges. Deal 1 damage per charge. If two different energy types are stored, describe the resulting blast as something wild (cosmetic only).
