---
name: Floppy Fish Club
type: weapon
tagline: A large dried fish, flopped limply in your hand. It is indestructible.
effect: "+2 attack against unarmored opponents. Cannot be destroyed by any means."
consumable: false
---

It is a fish. It is large. It flops. It feels like it should smell bad but somehow does not. Swinging it at something produces a sound that is extremely undignified.

It cannot be cut, burned, bent, torn, dissolved, or otherwise damaged. Scholars who have examined it (none, actually — it has never left the island) would have no explanation for this.

**GM note:** The weapon looks useless enough that opponents don't take it seriously. That is the correct response. They are wrong.
