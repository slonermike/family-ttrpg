# Legendary Items

Earned through story moments, boss victories, or major moral choices — never drawn randomly.

## Persistent Legendaries (restore at end of battle)

| Item | Description | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Dawnshard Blade | Sword of pure light that banishes shadows | Strike two enemies with one swing |
| Spear of Piercing Truth | Ancient spear that strikes through any defense | Damage enemies directly, ignoring their armor (enemy armor is not destroyed/used up) |
| Hammer of Shattering | Massive hammer for crushing blows | Deal 2 damage to a single enemy with one strike |
| Bow of Desperate Flame | Cursed bow of immense but dangerous power | Roll d20 as many times as you want on your attack — but if you roll a 1, the bow is consumed in fire and you take 1 damage (flip an ability) |
| Guardian Veil | Protective artifact watching over companions | Absorbs the next hit to ANY party member (your choice who). Restored to you at end of battle |
| Breastplate of Thorns | Armor covered in sharp vengeful spikes | When you are hit, this armor is destroyed AND the attacker takes 1 damage |
| Balloon Pants of Buoyancy | Ridiculous inflatable trousers | Use a turn to inflate or deflate. While inflated, you float above battle — only ranged weapons can hit or be used by you |

## Consumable Legendaries (permanently used after activation)

| Item | Description | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Martyr's Mantle | Sacred cloak of ultimate sacrifice | Destroy this to flip ALL party members' abilities face-up (full heal everyone) — CONSUMABLE |
| Stone of Restoration | Sacred stone that mends all protection | Roll to use — if successful, flip ALL party members' armor face-up — CONSUMABLE |
| Star Shard | Fragment of celestial perfection | Before any roll, use this to automatically roll a natural 20 — CONSUMABLE |
| Fire from Heaven | Holy flame called down from the sky | Roll to attack — if successful, deal 1 damage to ALL enemies in battle — CONSUMABLE |
| Chains of Binding | Ethereal chains that hold evil at bay | Roll to attack one enemy — if successful, that enemy cannot act until all other enemies are defeated — CONSUMABLE |
| Scroll of Provision | Ancient words that call forth what is needed | Roll to use — if successful, every party member draws one card from the Items deck — CONSUMABLE |
