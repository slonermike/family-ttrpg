A large woman from the waist up — except the head is a carp's: round goggle eyes, green-gold scales, and an enormous hinged jaw lined with needle fangs. Below the waist: Barbie legs, perfectly plastic pink, ending in tiny pointed feet. She stands in the shallows looking completely unbothered by any of this.

**Paralyzing Bite:** On a hit, skip HP damage — the target is bitten and paralyzed, landing face-down in the stream. Paralysis lasts several minutes (narrative: that player is out for the rest of the encounter). The water is six inches deep. They can breathe. They cannot move. They are lying face-down in a stream.

**In her element:** She moves easily in water. If lured onto dry land, her Barbie legs give her trouble — lower attack threshold to 6.

**She doesn't want to fight.** She wants you to go away, or bring her something clean, beautiful, and stinky. Combat is the consequence of a choice the party made.
