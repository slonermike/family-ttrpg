Something enormous. Hunched. It breathes in long pulls that move through the whole chamber. It has been in this cave for a very long time and has had nothing to do with that time but be angry about it.

It has no eyes. It never did. It navigates by sound, air pressure, and the faint heat of living things. It is extremely good at this.

**Suppressed Light:** In Blind Anger's presence, a torch throws light no further than five feet. This is not magic — it's him. Something about the density of his attention on the dark collapses ordinary light. The only exception is the locket he carries, which he cannot suppress because he cannot open it.

**Spotting the Locket:** He wears a locket around his neck — :item[illuminating-truth]. In torch-light (five-foot radius), spotting it requires **Insight or Wit 12+** as an action. In the locket's own light (after it's retrieved), it was obvious all along. Until then, it's just a gleam at the edge of dark.

**He fights by sound.** Loud players — yelling, clanging metal, stomping — give him +2 to his next attack. Quiet players (whispered coordination, soft footfalls) can strip that back. This isn't a hard rule; use it to reward players who think about it.

**Resists Might:** You cannot out-muscle him. He is larger and angrier than anything the party can match with raw force. Agility, Wit, Insight, Heart, and Creation all work normally.

**If Murn and Goob are present:** They track every movement, breath, and shift in weight. Each party member gets one free re-roll on any attack or defense roll this encounter. When a player uses theirs, they describe what a Wumble whispered just before the moment.

**If Murn and Goob are NOT present:** The suppressed torchlight works against the party — Blind Anger's attack threshold drops to 12 (he's practiced in this dark; they're not).

**When he falls:** Slowly. Not like something that gives up — like something that finally gets to rest. The locket falls with him.

He was not always just this. He doesn't know what else he was. He ran out of that a long time ago.
