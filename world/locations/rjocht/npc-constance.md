Constance was captain of the city guard when Dorogh came. She rallied the guards to fight. Most died or were twisted into his service. She was captured, not killed.

Dorogh keeps her alive deliberately. He parades her before the people — chained, in tattered armor — as proof that resistance is futile. She has not broken. She sits upright. Head unbowed. Every time he presents her to the people as proof of his power, she gives them something else instead: evidence that you can keep your dignity under the worst pressure.

She sits in a basement cell beside the entrance to the Keep. A barred window at ground level. Chains. She is still wearing her armor. It is tattered. She wears it.

## Scene 5 — The Discovery

Party approaches the heavy iron doors of the Keep (flanked by 2 Corrupted Guards). Before they reach it, Constance's voice — weak but defiant — comes from the barred window. A moment to speak before the guards notice.

**First words:** *"Who...? You're not from here. [Eyes fix on the Blade] Is that... the Blade of Righteous Judgment? By the King, it IS. Then it's finally happening. The Order hasn't forgotten us after all."*

## Crucial Intelligence She Gives

She tells the party what she knows:
- Dorogh seems invincible. She has watched warriors charge him. Nothing touches him.
- But she SAW him, not with her eyes but with her heart. The corruption eating at him, the lies Drachan fed him. For a moment she saw the man he used to be.
- That is when he struck her down and screamed: *"Don't look at me like that!"*
- Truth itself was a weapon. **If they can see through his corruption — truly SEE him — that is when he is vulnerable. That is when the Blade can strike.**
- His guards will come first. Save strength.

**Mechanically:** This is the hint that the Insight ability reveals Dorogh's vulnerability and triggers Phase 2.

## The Choice (Scene 5)

**FREE HER NOW (loud):**
- Might 14+ to smash chains, or Lockpicks 12+
- Guards hear it; combat starts immediately
- Constance fights with the party (weakened: -2 to rolls, gives party +1 inspiration)
- After battle: *"Thank you. Now go. I'll guard the door."*

**PROMISE TO RETURN (quiet):**
- She says: *"Good. Smart. Don't waste strength on me. I've waited this long.*
  *Promise me: when you strike him down, remember he wasn't always a monster. Show him mercy at the end if you can. Let him remember what he was."*

**GM Note:** Her request for mercy is honored — not by sparing Dorogh, but by the Blade delivering judgment with dignity. His final whisper (*"Thank you... I remember... the gardens..."*) is that mercy. She asked for him to remember what he was. He does.

## Eucatastrophe (Scene 8)

When Dorogh falls, Constance breaks her chains and leads the people of Rjocht out into the streets. She does not give a speech. She walks out and people follow.
