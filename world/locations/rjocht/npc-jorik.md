Jorik runs a smithy near the inner wall in Rjocht's eastern district. He stayed when others left or were driven out. He is still here, still making things, still giving what he can to people who need it.

Under Dorogh, a blacksmith is useful — weapons and chains must be maintained. Jorik has walked that line with integrity, making what he must and protecting who he can.

He is not impressive to look at. He is enormous, soot-covered, and chooses his words like he's spending something precious.

**Note:** The scene 2 PDF draft used the name "Kael." Jorik is canon — confirmed in session 1 debrief.

## GM Notes

- Give him weight. When Jorik speaks, slow down.
- He gave the party a Healing Potion without asking for anything in return.
- He told them about Dorogh — not the military details, but the human cost. The people. What the city used to be.
- He can serve as a safe return point inside Rjocht if the party needs a place to rest or regroup.

**Voice:** "The fog's been here longer than I can remember. But fog doesn't last forever. Nothing does."

## Session 8 Callback

Among the first people out the door when the fog lifts. He doesn't say much. He just stands in the light with his eyes closed.
