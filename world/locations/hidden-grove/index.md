Ancient forest deep enough that the sky disappears. Massive trees grow so close their branches interlock above, forming a cathedral ceiling. Light filters through golden — not sunlight exactly, something older and warmer.

The air here does not feel empty. It hums. Children might describe it as the sound of something listening.

This is where Elder Greymane conducted the gifting ceremony before the campaign began. The party received their ability cards and starting items here. The :item[blade-of-righteous-judgment] was passed to MuhDude.

The Grove cannot be found by searching. Paths through the forest only open when the Spirit of Life allows. It is not a place you go to — it is a place you are brought to.

## Scene Role

Opening ceremony location. Establishes:
- The party's calling and gifting
- The weight of the Blade
- That something larger is at work (Deep Magic subtext — do not explain)

## Key Moment

Elder Greymane lifts the Blade and places it in MuhDude's hands without ceremony or fanfare. Says nothing for a long beat. Then: *"You were made for this."*

## Session 8 Callback

After Dorogh's fall, Elder Greymane arrives in Rjocht and the scene echoes the Grove — golden light, something listening again. The city remembers what sunlight looks like.
