


# Ability Reference Guide

## MIGHT - Strength & Protection

**COMBAT:** Attack with swords/hammers, block with shields, grapple enemies, smash defenses
**EXPLORATION:** Break down doors, move heavy objects, clear paths through debris
**FRIENDLY ENCOUNTERS:** Impress with strength, protect others physically, help lift/move things
**OBSTACLES:** Hold up collapsing structures, break chains/bonds, smash through barriers
**SPIRITUAL/MAGICAL:** Stand firm against spiritual pressure, resist physical curse effects

---

## AGILITY - Speed & Finesse

**COMBAT:** Dodge attacks, shoot bows/throw weapons, quick strikes, move to better positions
**EXPLORATION:** Climb walls/trees/cliffs, sneak quietly, balance on ledges, swim, squeeze through tight spaces
**FRIENDLY ENCOUNTERS:** Perform acrobatics to entertain, catch falling people, move gracefully
**OBSTACLES:** Disarm traps quickly, catch falling objects, jump across gaps
**SPIRITUAL/MAGICAL:** Dodge magical attacks, escape spiritual bindings through nimbleness

---

## INSIGHT - Spiritual Sensing

**COMBAT:** Sense hidden enemies, detect undead/corruption, see through illusions, anticipate attacks spiritually
**EXPLORATION:** Feel the Deep Magic's presence, sense danger early, find blessed/cursed places
**FRIENDLY ENCOUNTERS:** Discern lies/truth, sense emotional/spiritual states, diagnose sickness/curses
**OBSTACLES:** Perceive spiritual puzzles, sense the right path, understand ancient symbols
**SPIRITUAL/MAGICAL:** Resist mental attacks, communicate with the Deep Magic, break/understand curses, detect evil

---

## WIT - Clever Thinking

**COMBAT:** Spot weaknesses, devise tactics, set traps, outsmart enemies instead of overpowering
**EXPLORATION:** Notice hidden doors/passages, track/read clues, decipher maps, search for hidden items
**FRIENDLY ENCOUNTERS:** Convince with clever words, notice what people care about, bargain/negotiate, tell riddles
**OBSTACLES:** Solve puzzles/riddles, figure out mechanisms, find creative solutions, disable complex traps
**SPIRITUAL/MAGICAL:** Understand magical patterns, break enchantments intellectually, outsmart corrupted creatures

---

## HEART - Compassion & Healing

**COMBAT:** Inspire allies, show mercy to enemies, rally frightened friends, encourage emotionally
**EXPLORATION:** Comfort scared animals, build trust with locals, sense emotional history of places
**FRIENDLY ENCOUNTERS:** Persuade through kindness, befriend strangers, calm angry/frightened people, make meaningful promises
**OBSTACLES:** Encourage team through challenges, keep morale up during hardship
**SPIRITUAL/MAGICAL:** Heal after INSIGHT diagnosis, break curses through love, help resist oppression, lay on hands/speak blessings

---

## CREATION - The Natural World

**COMBAT:** Command animals to help, use environment as weapons (vines/terrain), befriend potential enemies
**EXPLORATION:** Find safe wilderness paths, identify useful plants/herbs, read weather/nature signs, track
**FRIENDLY ENCOUNTERS:** Speak with animals for information, understand creature needs, help injured animals
**OBSTACLES:** Grow climbing vines, ask animals for help, find helpful plants & water, create shelter from natural elements
**SPIRITUAL/MAGICAL:** Sense corruption in nature, call creation to respond to goodness, purify corrupted spaces, understand Deep Magic in creation

---

**Remember:** You add 2d6 (pick the higher one) when using an ability. If you have 2 of the same ability, add both d6 together instead!
